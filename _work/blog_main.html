New Balance Skateboard Team skates Vancouver

New Balance have recently gotten into the skate game with a wide selection of pro model shoes to offer. Check out their video The Second Narrows shot exclusively in Vancouver with pros Arto Saari, PJ Ladd, John Ratray, Marquise Henry among others.

DC Shoes collab with NVRBRKN. Opening Event

DC Shoes has recently teamed up with the clothing brand NVRBRKN to develop some dope ass sneakers. They recently had a pop up gallery at Fred Segal shop in LA. DC royalty such as Colin McKay and Danny Way showed up to the openning event to promote their freshest collaboration. Check out the video for highlights of the fun that ensued.

The World of Dance Tour made a stop in Vancouver last month to showcase the best of Vancouver's dance crews. Sneakerheadz were there to share in the action with a booth showing off some fresh kicks. B-boys and girls unite...AIGHT!
